My Experience – Methylene Blue (MB): How I Take It

Important Safety Note
Before I started using Methylene Blue, I had to work with my doctor to dose down off of Lexapro, the SSRI antidepressant I was taking. SSRIs and Methylene Blue do not mix well. That combination can cause Serotonin Toxicity (Serotonin Syndrome), which is very dangerous and can be life‑threatening. Also, some people have a genetic condition called G6PD deficiency, and Methylene Blue can cause serious problems for them as well. So please do your own research and talk with your doctor before doing anything like this.

How I Take It
I use the eye dropper that comes with it and drop it directly into my mouth and swallow. I do not mix it with anything. Mixing it with liquids can reduce the effectiveness of the Methylene Blue. I take 10 drops per dose, three times per day with my vitamins: Morning, Afternoon, and Night.

The Blue Dye Reality
MB is a bright blue dye. It will stain things. It can stain your countertop, sink, or toilet. It will also temporarily stain your lips, teeth, and tongue blue until it is absorbed by the body.

The reason I take it is because it can help deliver electrons directly to Complex IV in the electron transport chain, and it acts as a very powerful antioxidant right inside the ETC.
